Welcome to
Black Grouse Media



We have been producing commercial videos and professional photography for businesses for the best part of a decade and over that time have developed a vast amount of knowledge and experience around the best ways to produce high quality content to market businesses in the best way possible.In that time, we have also invested in some of the best equipment available including high end professional camera and sound equipment.

It's not about all the equipment obviously, so we have also invested huge amounts of time in education and training to develop our skills over the years to be able to use industry leading software such as Final Cut Pro X, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom and much more.

You can rest assured we do not cut corners on quality. That said, we have always ensured that our services have remained affordable for businesses of all sizes and we still work with many clients who were our first back when we started.

We also hold qualifications in learning and developing meaning that if you choose to take advantage of any of our learning opportunities, workshops or classes, you'll be assured of a worthwhile experience.

As for our home life we are huge animal lovers currently with four labradors, a terrier and a cocker spaniel (most of which are working gundogs) and we are also ridiculously enthusiastic formula 1 fans!

So whether you are a business in Kent or anywhere else in the world, we would love to chat with you about how we can help your business.Black Grouse Media is run by James and Maddi, a husband and wife team based near Canterbury in Kent.
